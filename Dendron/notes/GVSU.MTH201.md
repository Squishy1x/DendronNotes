### Approximating the Derivative

#### Formula:
$$
f'(a) ~= \frac {f(a + h) - f(a - h)} {2h}
$$

### Derivative Units
    Derivative units are always:
$$
\frac {output\space units} {input\space units}
$$

#### Example
$U'(4) = -2 ft^3/min$

    After 4 mins, losing 2 cubic feet per minute
